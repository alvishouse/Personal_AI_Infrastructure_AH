# Napkin Style

## Style Overview
**"Back of Napkin Sketch"**

Spontaneous sketch on paper napkin in ballpoint pen style. Quick, gestural, authentic brainstorm captured in 2-4 minutes during a strategic conversation. The raw, unpolished energy of breakthrough insights happening in real-time.

## Core Aesthetic
- **Single ballpoint pen** throughout (MANDATORY)
- **Napkin beige texture** visible everywhere
- **Gestural spontaneous energy** (drawn FAST)
- **Visible corrections** (scribbled out, NOT erased)
- **Hurried all-caps handwriting**
- Strategy session authenticity

## When to Use This Style
- Authentic strategic storytelling
- "Origin story" moments (where breakthrough happened)
- Vulnerable, unpolished thinking process
- Startup/entrepreneurial contexts
- Social media content showing real work
- Email: "Here's what I sketched on a napkin..."

---

## COLOR PALETTE

### Background
- **Napkin beige:** #F5F2E8
- Textured napkin surface visible throughout
- Compressed paper fiber grain
- Slight creases and natural folds
- Imperfect disposable napkin quality

### Primary Ink
- **Ballpoint blue:** #2C5F8D
- ONLY ink color used (single pen rule)
- Used for ALL elements:
  - All sketching
  - All text
  - All arrows
  - All annotations
- Variable pressure (darker where pressed hard)
- Bleeds slightly into napkin fibers

### DO NOT INCLUDE
- NO coffee stains or ring marks
- NO water marks or liquid stains
- Keep napkin clean except for ink

---

## ILLUSTRATION TECHNIQUE

### Line Quality (CRITICAL)
- **Quick confident ballpoint strokes**
- Gestural lines showing hand movement
- Lines have momentum (drawn fast)
- Variable pressure creates value
- Circles as spiraling overlapping loops
- Lines overshoot or connect imperfectly
- Scratchy quality on napkin texture
- 2-4 minute execution speed visible

### Single Tool Rule (MANDATORY)
- **ONLY ballpoint blue (#2C5F8D) used**
- NO other colors allowed
- NO highlighters, markers, or pencils
- This is CRITICAL for napkin authenticity
- Real napkin sketches use whatever pen is handy

### Corrections (CRITICAL)
- **1-2 visible corrections REQUIRED**
- Scribbled out with quick scratch marks (XXX)
- Arrow pointing to redrawn element
- Shows real-time thinking: "wait, that's not right..."
- Corrections NOT erased cleanly
- This is MANDATORY for authenticity

### Speed Indicators
- Can be sketched in 2-4 minutes
- 10-20 strokes for simple figures
- 20-30 strokes for cars or objects
- Abbreviations in tight spaces
- Some elements simplified due to speed

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Text Style
- **Hurried all-caps handwriting**
- Ballpoint pen (#2C5F8D) only
- Quick scratchy letters
- Readable but rushed
- Variable letter sizes
- Some labels squeezed in tight spaces

### Common Text Elements
- Labels with arrows pointing to subjects
- Circled emphasis words
- Underlines (single or double) for importance
- "!!!" or "!" emphasis marks
- Abbreviations natural (INFO, SYS, ACCEL, COLLAB)

---

## COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Layout Principles
- Organic placement (NOT planned)
- Uses 70-90% of napkin surface
- Some elements spill into margins
- Asymmetric, spontaneous
- No ground line or border

### Common Patterns
1. **Central Sketch:** Main idea occupies 60-70% of space
2. **Orbiting Labels:** Text scattered around naturally
3. **Quick Diagrams:** Simple before/after or flow
4. **Formula Sketches:** Icons with operators between them

### Gestural Arrows
- Quick arrows with single strokes
- Some with double-lines for emphasis
- Arrow heads drawn quickly (imperfect triangles)
- Show flow, connection, or pointing to labels

---

## CRITICAL STYLE CHARACTERISTICS

### Mandatory Elements
- Napkin texture visible throughout
- Single ballpoint pen (#2C5F8D) only
- Gestural spontaneous energy (fast)
- 1-2 visible corrections (scribbled out)
- Hurried all-caps handwriting
- Can be sketched in 2-4 minutes
- Ink bleeds into napkin fibers
- Imperfections are features

### Forbidden Elements
- Multiple pen colors
- Clean paper background
- Perfect circles or lines
- Typed fonts
- Erased corrections
- Careful planning
- Polished execution
- Digital polish
- Coffee stains or ring marks
- Water marks or liquid stains
