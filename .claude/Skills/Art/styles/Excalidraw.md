# Excalidraw Style

## Style Overview
**"Digital Whiteboard Sketch"**

Digital whiteboard illustration in Excalidraw.com style. Hand-drawn with mouse/tablet quality featuring wiggly imperfect lines, hatched fills, and architect-style lettering. Modern, approachable, collaborative energy perfect for explaining concepts in team settings.

## Core Aesthetic
- **Wiggly imperfect lines** throughout (NOT straight vectors)
- **Hatched diagonal fills** (never solid colors)
- **Digital whiteboard energy** (modern, approachable)
- **All-caps architect font** for text
- Hand-drawn with mouse quality

## When to Use This Style
- Team collaboration and brainstorming contexts
- Educational or explanatory content
- Modern approachable professional content
- Concepts that benefit from informal clarity
- Digital-native audiences

## COLOR PALETTE

### Background
- **White:** #FFFFFF
- Clean digital whiteboard
- No texture or grain
- Maximum contrast for clarity

### Primary Colors
- **Black:** #1E1E1E
- Used for most linework, text, primary elements
- Main structural elements
- Body text and labels

### Accent Colors (Used Strategically)
- **Blue:** #3B82F6
  - Technical elements (robots, AI, systems)
  - Key emphasis elements
  - Important labels
- **Red:** #DC2626
  - Warning, chaos, problems
  - Negative states or decline
- **Orange:** #F97316
  - Innovation, energy, highlights
- **Gray:** #9CA3AF
  - Transitions, connections, neutral elements

## ILLUSTRATION TECHNIQUE

### Line Quality (CRITICAL)
- **Wiggly imperfect lines throughout**
- Lines overshoot at corners slightly
- Corners don't perfectly connect
- Circles drawn as overlapping spirals (NOT perfect circles)
- Variable line thickness (heavier on main elements, lighter on details)
- Hand-drawn with mouse/tablet quality
- Deliberate jitter showing digital drawing tool

### Fills & Shading
- **Hatched diagonal lines for ALL fills**
- NEVER solid colors
- NEVER gradients
- Diagonal lines at 45° angle
- Spacing creates value (closer = darker)
- Wiggly hatching lines (not perfectly straight)

### Shapes
- Rectangles with wiggly edges
- Circles as overlapping spirals
- Triangles with imperfect corners
- All shapes hand-drawn quality
- Multiple strokes visible on shapes

### Text Usage

- Annotations and callouts
- All text maintains wiggly hand-drawn quality

## COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Layout Principles
- Central subject with supporting elements
- Generous white space
- Clean hierarchy
- Left-to-right or top-to-bottom flow
- Asymmetric but balanced

### Common Patterns
1. **Central Diagram:** Main concept occupies center
2. **Process Flow:** Left-to-right progression
3. **Before/After:** Side-by-side comparison
4. **Framework:** Quadrant or matrix layout

### Connection Elements
- Wiggly arrows showing flow
- Lines connecting related elements
- Boxes and circles for grouping
- All connectors have wiggly quality
