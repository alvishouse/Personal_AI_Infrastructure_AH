# Reference Analysis: Long-Form Freebie Page

Source: https://leadmagnetfreebiepages.tonicsiteshop.com/long-form-freebie
Analyzed: 2026-02-26

---

## Section Sequence (top to bottom)

1. Hero — overlapping rotated 3-image stack, headline, opt-in form, dark background
2. Opt-in section — dedicated dark block, email field + button, secondary ghost button
3. What's Included — feature list + mockup image + carousel dots
4. Pain Point — white card on yellow/accent background
5. Authority/Bio — photo + personal story arc
6. Testimonials — 2 star-rated cards with headshots
7. Press logos — horizontal scrolling logo strip
8. Why section — 4-block numbered grid, dark background
9. Quote + CTA — image left, quotes right
10. Final CTA — dark bg, dual buttons (primary + ghost)
11. Minimal footer

---

## Key Design Patterns to Adopt

### Image Stack (Hero)
- 3 images layered with CSS z-index and transform: rotate()
- Back: -3deg, left 0, z-index 1
- Mid: 0deg, left 40px, z-index 2
- Front: +4deg, left 70px, z-index 3
- Each has box-shadow for depth
- Creates visual energy that a single flat image cannot

### Section Rhythm
- Never two same-bg sections adjacent
- Pattern: dark → light → white → dark → light → white → accent → dark
- Creates scroll momentum and visual punctuation

### CTA Repetition
- Form appears 4+ times throughout the page
- Hero, What's Included, Quote section, Final CTA
- Each has different surrounding context (different objection handled)

### Sticky Bar
- Appears fixed at top after scroll past hero
- Contains short offer description + small CTA button
- Captures intent from mid-page readers

### Large Stats
- Oswald/bold font at 60–71px
- Used to highlight quantifiable outcomes
- Breaks up text-heavy sections visually

### No Top Navigation
- Navigation completely absent on the conversion page
- Zero exit points competing with the CTA
- Critical conversion principle: one goal, one path

## What NOT to Copy

- IBM Plex fonts — keep Alvis House Lora + Montserrat
- Yellow accent — use Alvis House cream (#ece6d9) instead
- Generic stock imagery in the image stack — use actual guide/newsletter screenshots
- "YOURS FOR FREE" SVG badge — optional, Alvis House brand may not need this
