## PHASE 1 → PHASE 2 HANDOFF CHECKLIST

Before moving to extraction, verify the cornerstone has:

### Content Elements
- [ ] **Hook** with contrarian statement + identity filter
- [ ] **Challenge** with 3 specific pain points
- [ ] **Opportunity** with 3-4 transformation promises
- [ ] **Expert Story** following 3S formula (Structure, Struggle, Solution)
- [ ] **Framework** with 3-5 named steps + examples for each
- [ ] **2 Research Studies** with full citations
- [ ] **5 Examples** (famous, client, personal, relatable, cautionary)
- [ ] **Myth/Mindset** section with truth reveal
- [ ] **Application Guide** with weekly plan + checklist
- [ ] **Close** with identity affirmation + single next step

### Extraction Readiness
- [ ] Each section is clearly delineated with headers
- [ ] Framework has a memorable name
- [ ] All examples have specific outcomes (numbers when possible)
- [ ] Hook contains multiple standalone statements
- [ ] Myth section has clear "from → to" mindset shift
- [ ] Total word count: 2,500-5,000 words

## LINKEDIN-SPECIFIC STRATEGY

### The Three Post Types (Revenue Focus)

| Type | % of Content | Purpose | Typical Reach | Revenue Potential |
|------|--------------|---------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Inspiration** | 20-30% | Build connection | 200K-2M | Low |
| **Engagement** | 20% | Feed algorithm | 50K-500K | Low |
| **Authority** | 50-60% | Generate revenue | 5K-50K | High |

### Authority Post Formula (The Money Maker)
```
SPECIFIC HOOK (with credibility signal)
+ FRAMEWORK (your system)
+ PROOF POINT (results/research)
+ SOFT CTA
= Revenue-generating post
```

### Hook Starters That Work
Start LinkedIn posts with one of these four words:
1. **"I"** / **"I've"** (personal credibility)
2. **"When"** (situational relevance)
3. **"If"** (identity filter)

### Writing Rules for LinkedIn
1. **Fifth-Grade Rule:** If a 5th grader can't understand it, simplify it
2. **Spacing:** Maximum 2 lines per paragraph, white space is your friend
3. **Banger Lines:** Every 2-3 sentences needs a line that could be screenshotted
4. **No Jargon:** Unless your audience uses it daily

### LinkedIn Image Strategy
- Hand-drawn/sketch style outperforms polished graphics
- Framework diagrams drive saves
- Before/after comparisons drive engagement
- Quote graphics drive shares

| Metric | Type | What It Tells You |
|--------|------|-------------------|
| Impressions | Vanity | How many saw it (not useful alone) |
| Likes | Vanity | Low-effort agreement |
| Comments | **Value** | Engaged audience, conversation starters |
| DMs | **Value** | High-intent prospects |
| Saves | **Value** | Content worth returning to |
| Profile visits | **Value** | Interest in you, not just content |
| Link clicks | **Value** | Action taken |
| Conversations started | **Value** | Business potential |

### The Real Success Metric
**Revenue per impression** > Impressions alone

8,000 impressions → 1 client → $25,000 
BEATS
2,000,000 impressions → 0 clients → $0
