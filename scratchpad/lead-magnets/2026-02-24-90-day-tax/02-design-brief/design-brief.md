## Style Direction

### What "Minimalist Clean" Means Here

Take the authoritative dark-navy tone from the PDF slides and strip away everything that isn't essential. Wide margins, generous white space, a single accent color, clean tables — no gradients, no textures, no decorative elements that don't carry meaning.

**Take from Template 1 (Content Marketing):**
- Light backgrounds with generous breathing room
- Modern open layout — content is never crowded
- Vibrant single accent color for emphasis

**Take from Template 2 (Red Staff Productivity):**
- Strong typographic hierarchy — bold weight does the heavy lifting
- Data presented in clean structured formats (tables, stat blocks)
- Premium, serious-business tone — nothing cute or casual

**Synthesized result:** White background, deep navy text, one bold accent blue, clean rules as dividers, oversized stat numbers as visual anchors.
