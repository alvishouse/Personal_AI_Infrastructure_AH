About This Guide

The invisible tax inside every AI leadership hire

Most organizations hire an AI leader and then hand them a blank canvas. No executive alignment. No validated use cases. No data readiness assessment. Just a title and a desk.

The result? Your most expensive hire spends their first 60–90 days doing what your organization should have done before they arrived — discovering the landscape, navigating politics, and building the foundation that should have been ready on Day 1.

That's the 90-Day Tax. It doesn't show up on a budget. It never gets questioned in a board meeting. But the true cost often exceeds $250,000 per hire — and it's entirely optional.

This guide shows you exactly what's happening, how to diagnose it in your own organization, and what to do about it.
