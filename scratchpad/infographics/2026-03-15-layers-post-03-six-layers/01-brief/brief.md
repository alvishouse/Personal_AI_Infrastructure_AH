## Core Message
AI fails because the foundation layers beneath it are weak — not because the technology is wrong.

## Target Audience
Enterprise leaders and AI practitioners evaluating why AI pilots stall.

## Key Statistics
- 80% of AI projects never reach full production value
- 15% of organizations have networks fully ready for AI (Cisco)
- 1% of firms have processes ready for full digital transformation benefits
- 96% of organizations hit data quality problems
- 50% of employees use unapproved AI tools
- 269 shadow AI tools per 1,000 staff
- Only 31% of organizations are talent-ready for AI
- 5.3x success rate with cultural investment in AI
- Focus on 3.5 use cases = 2.1x ROI vs. spreading across 6+

## Six Layers
1. Infrastructure Intelligence — Can your systems run, scale, and connect?
2. Data Intelligence — Structured, governed, accessible data?
3. Process Intelligence — Workflows defined, measured, repeatable?
4. Governance Intelligence — Guardrails in place?
5. People Intelligence — People know how to collaborate with AI?
6. Strategy Intelligence — AI aligned to business outcomes?
