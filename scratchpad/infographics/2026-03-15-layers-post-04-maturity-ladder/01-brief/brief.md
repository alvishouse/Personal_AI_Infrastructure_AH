# Infographic Brief — AI Maturity Ladder

## Series
**Layers & Ladders** — Post 04

## Core Message
99% of organizations are below Rung 4. Knowing your actual rung — not the aspirational one — changes every investment decision.

## Narrative Arc
**Pattern D — Data Story**

Problem (orange bar) → Evidence (stat strip + rung grid) → Solution (honest diagnostic)

## Audience
AI-curious leaders and practitioners on LinkedIn who believe they're further along the maturity curve than the data supports.

## Key Insight
The gap between "aspirational rung" and "actual rung" is the root cause of repeated investment failures. Most organizations feel like they're at Rung 3. Most are at Rung 2.
