99% of organizations are below Rung 4.

Most think they are at Rung 3.

Here is the hard truth: where you think you are and where you actually are rarely match. The maturity ladder has six rungs. Most organizations are stuck on one or two without knowing it. The data is clear on this.

Only 11% of organizations have agentic AI in full production. Only 1% describe their AI rollout as mature. The gap between "we use AI" and "AI is creating systematic value" is enormous — and most leaders underestimate it.

**1/ Rung 1 — Awareness (8% of organizations)**
Individuals experimenting on their own. No shared vocabulary. No formal strategy. Primary blocker: nobody has defined what winning looks like.

**2/ Rung 2 — Tool Adoption (39% — the largest cluster)**
AI tools introduced. Some individual productivity gains. No systemic integration. Primary blocker: Process Intelligence. Tools without workflows are isolated wins that never scale. This is the most dangerous rung — leaders assume adoption means progress.

**3/ Rung 3 — Process Integration**
AI embedded in defined workflows. KPIs tracked. Data pipelines strengthened. Primary blocker: the handoffs. The verification logic. The structured transition points between human and agent work.

**4/ Rung 4 — Operational Intelligence**
Cross-functional data integration. Formal governance. Repeatable AI deployment model. Primary blocker: People Intelligence — teams that can actually run what is built.

**5/ Rung 5 — Agentic Enablement (11% in full production)**
AI agents execute bounded decisions. Human oversight frameworks in place. Primary blocker: agent-level Process Intelligence — defined transition points and observable performance signals.

**6/ Rung 6 — Adaptive Enterprise (1% mature)**
Continuous learning loops. AI embedded in strategy itself. The destination most boards are asking about. The rung almost nobody has reached.

The lesson that matters: most organizations at Rung 2 believe they are at Rung 3. That one-rung error changes every investment decision you make next.

Be honest about your rung. Then fix the single blocker between you and the next one. Not all six at once. One blocker. One rung.

Which rung are you actually on?
