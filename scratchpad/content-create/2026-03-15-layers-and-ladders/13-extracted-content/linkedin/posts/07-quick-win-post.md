You do not need a consulting engagement.

You need four weeks and one honest question.

Most leaders stall on AI because the path forward feels like a $200,000 assessment and a six-month roadmap. It is not. The first move costs nothing. Here is the four-week plan to find your real constraint and fix it.

**Week 1 — Run a Layer Diagnostic.**
Score your organization on each of the six layers on a 1-to-5 scale: Infrastructure, Data, Process, Governance, People, Strategy. No consultant required. You already know where the weaknesses are — you just have not named them. Your lowest score is your ceiling. That is the layer blocking your next investment from working.

**Week 2 — Determine your actual rung.**
Most organizations are at Rung 1 or Rung 2 of the six-rung maturity ladder. Be honest. Rung 2 means tools are in place but processes are not. Rung 1 means individuals are experimenting but nothing is coordinated. Name your rung without defending it. You cannot fix what you will not face.

**Week 3 — Prescribe one intervention.**
Do not try to fix all six layers. Pick the single weakest layer blocking the next rung. One layer. One intervention. Trying to address everything at once is how organizations end up back at Rung 2 after spending $500,000.

**Week 4 — Define your first Flow Bridge.**
Pick one human-to-agent handoff in your operation. Define four things: the input artifact, the output artifact, the verification method, the escalation path. That is a Flow Bridge. Small. Specific. Foundation for everything that scales after it.

Three obstacles will come. Here is how to answer each.

- "We do not have budget." The Layer Diagnostic costs nothing. The Flow Bridge costs nothing. Real budget requests come after diagnostic evidence.
- "We do not have an executive sponsor." The diagnostic is your pitch. The weakest-layer finding names a specific problem and a specific next step.
- "We do not know where to start." You start with the Layer Diagnostic. That is the entire point of a diagnostic framework.

Four weeks. One honest diagnostic. One Flow Bridge.

That is the foundation everything else stands on.
