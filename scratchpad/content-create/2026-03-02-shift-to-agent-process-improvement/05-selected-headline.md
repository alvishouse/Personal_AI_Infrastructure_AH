## Final Headline

> The one architecture layer Lean never gave you — and why your AI agents keep failing without it

## 6-Part Build

- **How Many:** 1
- **The What:** The single missing layer between process improvement and AI agent performance
- **The How:** Architecture layer
- **The Who:** Lean and Six Sigma practitioners deploying AI
- **The Why:** Close the gap that explains why rigorous process shops still fail at AI
- **Twist the Knife:** Move from confused to credible when presenting AI strategy, stop treating agent failures as tool problems

## Why This Headline

"Lean never gave you" is a direct, respectful challenge to the reader's core professional identity. It does not say Lean is wrong. It says Lean is incomplete — a distinction that invites curiosity rather than defensiveness. The second clause connects the missing piece to a pain they are already experiencing daily (agents failing). This is the headline that makes a Six Sigma Black Belt stop scrolling.
