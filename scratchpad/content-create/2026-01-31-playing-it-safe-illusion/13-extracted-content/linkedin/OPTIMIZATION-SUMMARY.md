## Sanders CTA Strategy Breakdown

### The Stacked Approach

**Traditional Marketing:** Use one CTA (avoid confusion)
**Sanders Method:** Use multiple CTAs (each serves different function)

**Rationale:**
- Different audience members at different stages
- Some want to save (bookmark/send)
- Some want to engage (comment)
- Some want to share (repost)
- Offering all options = higher total conversion

### CTA Placement Strategy

**Top of Post (Resource/Viral Mechanic):**
- Catches readers who won't scroll to end
- Creates open loop ("I should comment DIAGNOSTIC later")
- Non-intrusive (parenthetical/italics)

**Middle/Bottom (Engagement Question):**
- Natural conclusion to argument
- Readers primed to engage after consuming content
- Question format = psychological trigger to respond

**End (Distribution Stack):**
- Final conversion opportunity
- Multiple actions = at least one resonates
- Visual icons make scanning easy
