### Voice Fixes Applied

**Fix 1 — Hedge word removed:**
Before: "Here is what the data says about where you probably are right now."
After: "Here is where most organizations are right now."

Rationale: "probably" hedges the authority. "what the data says" is throat-clearing before the actual insight. The declarative version lands harder and trusts the reader.

**Problem:** The identity filter was a buried prose sentence: "This piece is for the leader who has deployed AI tools and is staring at flat numbers."

**Fix:** Extracted and structured the identity filter with three challenge bullets and three transformation arrows immediately following the crack spread definition. The prose sentence became the entry point, challenges follow, transformations follow.

**Fix:** Added a Quick-Start Checklist after the Week 4 section, before Common Obstacles. Three preparation items + three first-24-hours actions.
